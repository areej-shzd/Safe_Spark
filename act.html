“KNOWING WHAT TO DO DURING A SEIZURE CAN SAVE A LIFE — STAY CALM, ACT SAFELY, AND BE PREPARED"

Find some first aid seizure protocols you need to know!

Hover over the boxes to view the different protocols and steps you can take when a person is experiencing a siezure!

Be Calm

Don’t panic; most seizures are not emergencies

Time the Seizure

If it lasts more than 5 minutes, call emergency services

Protect from Injury

Clear the area, cushion the head, and loosen tight clothing

Turn the Person on Their Side

Helps keep the airway clear and prevents choking

Don't restrain and restrict

Never restrain or Put Anything in Their Mouth – This can cause injury.

Stay Until it's Over

Offer reassurance and stay with the person until they're fully awake.
